CARMEN TODAY

Ever since the house was founded in 1875, Carmen has continuously defined British French luxury
on the basis of its core values of quality, refinement and innovation. In addition to its outstanding tanzanite high end jewellery collections,
Carmen has grown to take a premiere position in jewellery industry and also offers the most exquisite timepieces by partnering
recently with Bovet 1822 and Rolex. Carmen will continue to innovative and offer the highest level of differentiated design
and craftmanship in diversified product mix of Silver, Crystal, China,
Leather, Jewellery and Timepieces

OUR COMPANY

Our Company For over five decades, royalty and international collectorshave composed
the clientele of the esteemed British jewellery brand which David Morris established in 1962.
Jeremy Morris has since followed in his father's footstep and is now managing director and principal designer of David Morris.
From within the elegant New Bond Street atelier, in the heart of London, beautiful couture jewellery is hand crafted
to the highest standars using only the finest stones to ensure each unique pieces is a work of art.
